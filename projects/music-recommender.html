The Challenge

Music streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music) face a fundamental problem: discovery at scale. With millions of songs and users, how do you recommend the right track to the right person at the right time?

Traditional approaches fail when there's limited user-song interaction data. The Music Recommender project aimed to build a robust system that handles both popular and niche music using hybrid recommendation techniques.

The Technical Solution

The system combines collaborative filtering, content-based filtering, and matrix factorization to generate recommendations.

User-Song Interaction Matrix
↓
Collaborative Filtering (CF)
Content-Based Filtering (CBF)
↓
Matrix Factorization (SVD)
↓
Cosine Similarity Scores
Cold-Start Handling (Content)
↓
Top K Recommendations

Key Features:

  • SVD Matrix Factorization: Decomposes the user-song matrix to discover hidden features.
  • Cold-Start Solution: For new users, content-based fallback ensures recommendations even without interaction history.
  • Scalable & Fast: Optimized NumPy/Pandas for real-time inference on large datasets.

The Results & Impact

  • Precision@10 Score: 0.78 on held-out test set (strong by industry standards).
  • Cold-Start Handling: Successfully recommends songs to 100% of new users.
  • Serendipity Score: 0.65: 65% of recommendations are novel to the user (discovery!).
  • Inference Latency: <50ms even for large catalogs with millions of songs.

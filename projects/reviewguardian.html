The Challenge

E-commerce sellers and marketplace operators face a critical threat: malicious reviews. Competitors, coordinated actors, and bots post fake 1-star or 5-star reviews to manipulate product rankings or damage reputations. Traditional review moderation is manual, slow, and prone to errors.

The goal was to create an intelligent system that detects suspicious review patterns—bots, coordinated attacks, and fake accounts—without requiring extensive labeled training data.

The Technical Solution

ReviewGuardian combines behavioral analysis, NLP anomaly detection, and graph-based clustering to identify suspicious review patterns without heavy supervision.

Key innovations:

  • Zero-Labeled Learning: Uses isolation forests and density estimation instead of supervised classification.
  • Cross-Platform Detection: Identifies coordinated campaigns across multiple accounts/platforms.
  • Temporal Awareness: Flags synchronized review posting (bot behavior).

The Results & Impact

  • 70%+ Detection Rate on synthetic bot campaigns, minimal false positives.
  • Real-Time Alerting: Suspicious reviews flagged within minutes of posting.
  • Marketplace Trust: Businesses can now focus on genuine customer feedback.
  • Scalable & Adaptable: Easily extended to new platforms and review types.
